Research & Strategy

Our research & strategy team works towards marrying both your business goals and customer needs. They go deep into understanding your sector, identifying market trends and unlocking opportunities that elevate your customer's journey and experience.

Activities include:

  • Usability testing
  • Stakeholder interviews/workshops
  • Sector and competitor analysis
  • Customer journey mapping

Digital Product & Design

From websites and apps to full-blown digital transformation projects, our design process sees us create, test and iterate. We focus on user experience and user interface to deliver products that meet customer expectations and client objectives.

Activities include:

  • UX prototyping & testing
  • Service blueprints
  • Digital first branding
  • UI & Interaction design

Technical Delivery

Technical delivery is at the heart of our business. We pride ourselves on designing and delivering efficient, robust and scalable applications.

Activities include:

  • Open-source and enterprise CMS, CRM and DAM solutions
  • Large-scale custom-built portal and commerce systems
  • Responsive, progressive and accessible client-side applications
  • Use of innovative technologies like AR, Bots, Voice, AI and ML
  • API Development and Integrations with third-party, legacy and proprietary systems

Highly available cloud-hosted web, serverless and containerised applications

Data Intelligence & Optimisation

It's one thing to collect data, it's another to make sense of it. Numbers tell stories if you know what to look for and can connect the dots. Our data analysts and optimisation team use a range of tools to provide insights and recommendations that should impact your bottom line.

Activities include:

  • Customer segmentation
  • Transactional data modelling
  • Web analytics & insight
  • Optimisation & A/B testing
